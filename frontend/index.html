About Us

What is StrokeGuard AI?

StrokeGuard is a cutting-edge health platform dedicated to reducing the global burden of stroke. Every 40 seconds, someone in the world has a stroke — and many are preventable with early detection.

By combining state-of-the-art machine learning with clinical expertise, we provide personalised early warning signs and actionable health insights — completely free.
